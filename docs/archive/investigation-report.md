## Implication for Texture Work

Since USD is auto-generated from URDF:
- The generated USD inherits URDF's flat gray material — no textures
- STL meshes have no UV coordinates — cannot receive texture maps
- Any material/texture work must happen AFTER USD generation, or via a parallel Blender/DCC pipeline
